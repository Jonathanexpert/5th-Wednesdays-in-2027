📌 Understanding 5th Wednesdays in 2027

A fifth Wednesday occurs when a month has 31 days and the first day falls on a Wednesday, or when the month has 30 days and starts on Tuesday/Wednesday accordingly — 2027 delivers exactly four months with an extra Wednesday: June, September, and December (and also March? ✅ June 2027 — starts on Tuesday, has 30 days → gives 5 Wednesdays (2,9,16,23,30).
✅ September 2027 — starts on Wednesday, 30 days → 5 Wednesdays (1,8,15,22,29).
✅ December 2027 — starts on Wednesday, 31 days → 5 Wednesdays (1,8,15,22,29).
